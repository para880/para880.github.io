Your privacy is important to Anabas. This policy statement describes Anabas' treatment of personally identifiable information obtained through our website. This policy also covers Anabas' treatment of any personally identifiable information that Anabas' partners share with Anabas.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Anabas does not own or control, or to people that Anabas does not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Usage
In general, you can visit Anabas' website without telling us who you are and without revealing any information about yourself. There are times, however, when we may need information from you.

You may choose to give us some of your personal information, such as your email address and your name, for example, for us to correspond with you, to process an order or to provide you with a subscription or service.

Our Web server may record information it receives from your browser including your IP address, Operating System and browser type and version. We may use this information to customize our site, products, and/or services.

It is our policy that

  • Anabas will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
  • Anabas will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people only when
      * We have your consent to share the information;
      * We need to share the information to provide the product or service you have requested;
      * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process.


    Changes to Privacy Policy
    Anabas will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our webpages if there are substantial changes in the way we use your personal information.
